Gintama

Studio: Sunrise

Episodes: 367

Status: Finished

Summary: Set in an alternate-history Edo period, Gintoki Sakata, a freelancer, tackles odd jobs with his friends in a world where aliens have invaded. Chaos and comedy follow.

The adventures of this unlikely trio are packed with ridiculous humor, insane action, and touching moments that will leave you laughing and crying.

Konosuba: God's Blessing on This Wonderful World!

Studio: Studio Deen

Episodes: 20 + OVAs + Movie

Status: Ongoing

Summary: Kazuma, a teenager who dies an untimely death, is reincarnated into a fantasy world with a group of dysfunctional adventurers. Hilarity ensues as they bumble through quests.

This isekai comedy focuses on their misadventures, blending laugh-out-loud humor with over-the-top fantasy antics.
